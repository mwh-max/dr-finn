How to Apply

There are three ways to apply for Indiana Medicaid. All three go to the same place — FSSA reviews your application no matter how you submit it.

Online — Fastest Option

Available 24/7

Apply online through the FSSA benefits portal. You can apply for Medicaid along with other benefits like SNAP food assistance and TANF.

You will need to upload documents or mail them after you apply.

By Phone

Mon–Fri, 8am–4:30pm ET

Call the FSSA Benefits Helpline: 800-403-0864

A caseworker will walk you through the application. Have your documents ready — see the Document Checklist.

In Person

Division of Family Resources (DFR)

Visit your local DFR office. Staff can help you fill out the application in person. Bring your documents — see the Document Checklist.

What Happens After You Apply?

Most decisions are made within 45 days. If your application involves a disability determination, it may take up to 90 days.

You will get a letter in the mail with the decision. If approved, your coverage usually starts the first day of the month you applied.
