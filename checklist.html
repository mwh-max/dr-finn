Document Checklist

You do not need everything on this list. Gather what applies to your situation before you call or visit a DFR office.

Proof of Identity

One of these

  • Driver’s license or state ID
  • U.S. passport
  • Birth certificate
  • Social Security card
  • Military ID

Proof of Indiana Residency

One of these

  • Recent utility bill (electric, gas, water)
  • Lease or rental agreement
  • Mortgage statement
  • Mail addressed to you at your Indiana address

Proof of Income

All that apply

  • Recent pay stubs (last 4 weeks)
  • Employer letter stating wages and hours
  • Self-employment records or tax return
  • Unemployment benefit statements
  • Social Security or SSI award letter
  • Pension or retirement income statements
  • Child support or alimony documentation

If you have no income, you may need to sign a statement saying so.

Household Information

For everyone in your home

  • Names and dates of birth for all household members
  • Social Security numbers (if available)
  • Immigration documents, if applicable

Health Insurance Information

If you have or recently had coverage

  • Current health insurance card and policy number
  • Any employer-offered insurance details

Even if you have some insurance, you may still qualify for Medicaid.

Special Situations

Bring these if they apply

  • Pregnant: doctor’s confirmation of pregnancy
  • Disability: SSI or SSDI award letter, or disability documentation
  • Foster youth: documentation of foster care history
