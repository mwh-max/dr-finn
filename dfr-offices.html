Local Help

DFR Offices

Indiana’s Division of Family Resources (DFR) offices handle Medicaid applications, renewals, and questions. There is a DFR office in every county.

Find Your DFR Office

FSSA DFR Office Directory →

Major Metro Offices

  • Evansville (Vanderburgh County): 500 E. Walnut St, Evansville, IN 47713
  • South Bend (St. Joseph County): 227 W. Jefferson Blvd, South Bend, IN 46601

Addresses are approximate. Verify before visiting.
