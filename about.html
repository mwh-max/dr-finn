About Indiana Medicaid Notebook

What This Is

Indiana Medicaid Notebook is a free, independent guide to help Hoosiers understand their Medicaid options. It was built to answer the questions people most often have — in plain language, without jargon or government-speak.

This site is not affiliated with the State of Indiana, FSSA, or any insurance company.

Who Made It

This site was made by Sleeping Bear Labs, a small civic technology studio.

Sleeping Bear Labs also made the Kentucky Medicaid Notebook.

Meet Dr. FINN

Dr. FINN — Firefly Indiana Notebook Navigator — is our mascot. Dr. FINN is a Say’s Firefly, Indiana’s official state insect, named after Thomas Say, a Hoosier naturalist. Like a firefly, Dr. FINN is here to light the way through a confusing system.

Accuracy and Updates

We do our best to keep this information current and accurate. Indiana Medicaid rules and income limits change each year. Always verify important details with FSSA at 800-403-0864 before making decisions about your health coverage.
